Research Interests

Investigate the magmatic evolution of the mid-ocean ridge systems using mineralogical, geochemical, radio isotope, and geochemical simulation methods.

Explore the influence of slow spreading mid-ocean ridge tectonic environment on magmatic system, such as the migration and storage of magma under the detachment fault and NTD tectonic environment.

Constrain the crystallization pressure (depth) through major element barometer and vapor-saturation pressure derived from olivine-hosted melt inclusions.
